# MindBridge · MindJump · Rescue+

## Open Global Infrastructure for Inclusive AI, Automation and Assistive Robotics

Author: Alexander Rojas De La Riva  
Version: 1.1  
Year: 2026  

---

## Vision

MindBridge, MindJump and Rescue+ propose an open, human-centered technological infrastructure where:

- AI teaches
- Automation assists
- Robotics supports
- Every person can generate economic value

The objective is inclusion, dignity and productivity — not replacement of humans.

---

## Components

### MindBridge
Collaborative intelligence and practical AI education.

### MindJump
Automation tools for digital production and micro-income generation.

### Rescue+
Social and assistive layer focused on vulnerable and disabled populations.

---

## Principles

- Open and transparent architecture
- Low-cost accessibility
- Human-in-the-loop systems
- Assistive, not substitutive technology
- Replicable locally and globally
